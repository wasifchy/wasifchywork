  • SEMrush : Provides data on competitors’ SEO.

  • SimilarWeb : Offers insights into competitors’ website traffic.

  • SpyFu : Provides data on competitors’ keywords, rankings

Website Optimization

Tools:
UI/UX Analysis
  • Hotjar : Offers heatmaps, session recordings, and surveys to understand how users interact with your website
  • Crazy Egg : Provides heatmaps and visitor insights to help improve user experience and conversion rates.
  • Optimizely : An experimentation platform that allows you to A/B test different elements of your website.
  • Mouseflow : Mouseflow: Records users’ activity and provides heatmaps to give insights into user behavior.
WordPress Optimization
  • WP Rocket : A caching plugin that speeds up loading time by creating cached copies of each dynamically-generated page.
  • Smush : Image optimization plugin that compresses images without losing quality, helping your site load faster.
  • W3 Total Cache : Improves SEO and user experience by increasing website performance and reducing load times via features like content delivery network (CDN) integration and the latest best practices.
  • MonsterInsights : Integrates Google Analytics with your WordPress site, providing insights to make data-driven decisions.
Page Speed Tools
  • Google PageSpeed Insights : Analyzes the content of a web page, then generates suggestions to make that page faster.
  • GTmetrix : Provides insight into how well your site loads and gives actionable recommendations on optimization.
  • Pingdom : Tests the load time of a page and identifies bottlenecks.
Chrome Extensions
  • Lighthouse : An open-source, automated tool for improving the quality of web pages, including performance audits.
  • Page Load Time : Displays page load time in the toolbar to monitor and analyze performance.
  • Wappalyzer : Identifies software on the websites you visit, helping you to discover the technologies your competitors are using.
  • BuiltWith : Find out what websites are built with by analyzing the technology stack.
  • Check My Links : A link checker that crawls through your webpage and looks for broken links.

IG Management & Strategy

  • Buffer : Schedule posts, track the performance of your content, and manage all your Instagram marketing.
  • Sprout Social : Offers powerful scheduling, publishing, engagement tools, and detailed analytics for Instagram.

IG Content Creation
  • Canva : Offers a plethora of Instagram post and story templates to create eye-catching content.

IG Influencer Marketing

  • Heepsy : A tool for finding Instagram influencers by categories, location, engagement, and more.
IG Engagement Tools
  • Kicksta : An Instagram growth service that helps you get more organic followers by liking posts on your behalf.

Facebook Marketing

Tools:
FB Marketing Tools
  • Buffer : It’s a social media management tool that enables you to schedule posts, track the performance of your content, and manage all your accounts in one place​​.
  • Hootsuite : Another social media management platform, Hootsuite provides a variety of features including scheduling posts, tracking social ROI, running social media ads, and more​​.
  • Sprout Social : This tool provides social media management, analytics, and reporting features to help you understand and improve your social media performance​​.
  • SocialBee : Provides features like content categorization, evergreen posting, and performance analytics to help manage and optimize your social media efforts​​.
  • CoSchedule : It’s a marketing suite that includes social media management, content marketing, and email marketing tools​​.
FB Page Analyzation
  • Rival IQ : This tool allows for competitive benchmarking, Facebook ad analytics, and head-to-head reports comparing your page performance to competitors’​​.
  • Meta Business Suite : It’s a free tool by Facebook for managing ads, commerce, and content publishing on both Facebook and Instagram, with basic analytics features for reviewing insights about your posts and Page​​.
  • Keyhole : Provides sentiment analysis, recommendations for increasing followers, engagement, or conversion, along with hashtag and keyword tracking for understanding trends and optimizing engagement​​.
  • SocialPilot : Customizable, helps in scheduling, and discovering content for your business page, alongside analyzing posts from the system and other sources​.

Sales Funnels

Tools:
Sales Funnel Builders
  • ClickFunnels : An integrated platform for creating a series of web pages that work together to drive visitors toward a final conversion point.
  • Kartra : A multi-functional system providing not just funnel creation but also hosting and email service, albeit with a steeper learning curve.
  • Builderall : An expansive set of tools for online business management, including funnel building, though it may overwhelm with its breadth over depth.
  • Thrive Themes : Primarily a WordPress plugin suite focused on building conversion-friendly websites with a need for WP-centric users.
  • GrooveFunnels : A comprehensive digital marketing suite in growth mode, promising much but with some features still in development.
CRM and Sales Management
  • Infusionsoft by Keap : Combines CRM with sales and marketing, but can intimidate with its complexity and pricing.
  • Salesflare : A minimalist CRM that automates data entry, though it might not suffice for larger teams with complex sales processes.
  • Zoho CRM : Part of Zoho’s suite offers CRM customization, but might require additional Zoho apps for full functionality.
Analytics and Optimization Tools
  • Crazy Egg : Provides heatmaps and user insights, but faces stiff competition from more advanced analytics tools.
  • Funnelytics : Maps and analyzes funnels effectively, yet it requires existing traffic to yield insights.
  • Hotjar : Offers invaluable heatmaps and feedback tools, but is better for optimization post-funnel construction.

Google Analytics

Tools:
GA4 Chrome Extensions
  • Page Analytics : Allows you to see how customers interact with your web pages, including what they click and don’t click.
  • URL Builder : This extension lets you quickly add campaign parameters to URLs so you can track Custom Campaigns in Google Analytics.
  • Google Analytics Debugger : Turns on the debug version of Google Analytics JavaScript for all sites you browse using Chrome and logs errors, warnings, and hits.
GA4 WordPress Plugins
  • MonsterInsights : A plugin that connects your WordPress site to Google Analytics and allows you to view key analytics reports directly in your WordPress dashboard.
  • Analytify : Simplifies Google Analytics in WordPress and shows understandable analytics stats to help you make informed decisions.
  • ExactMetrics : Formerly Google Analytics Dashboard for WP, this plugin brings detailed reports and real-time statistics right into your WordPress site.
  • GA Google Analytics : Enables Google Analytics for your entire WordPress site with simple integration.

Google Ads

Tools:
Google Ads Optimization
  • WordStream : Offers tools to help manage and optimize Google Ads campaigns, often providing simpler analysis and actionable suggestions compared to the Google Ads interface.
  • SEMrush : A powerful digital marketing tool that, among other features, provides insights into your Google Ads strategies and those of your competitors.
Keyword Research
  • Google Keyword Planner : Integrated with Google Ads, this tool helps to find the right keywords to target for display ads, search ads, video ads, and app ads.
  • SpyFu : A competitor keyword research tool for Google Ads that allows you to see every keyword your competitors have bought on Google Ads and every ad test they’ve run.
  • Ahrefs : While known for SEO, Ahrefs also provides tools and insights that can be valuable for researching keywords for Google Ads.
Bid Management and Strategy
  • Optmyzr : Provides advanced PPC management tools to optimize bids, track budgets, and improve Quality Score in Google Ads.
  • Marin Software : An ad management solution for optimizing your Google Ads spending and performance.
